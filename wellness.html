Self Care

Self care is good for you! It helps manage stress, and overall health. There are many benefits to self care such as a goodnight rest, you are more focus, its boost immunity, regulates emotions and etc.

Ways you can practice self care :

- Get 7 to 9 hours of sleep

- Eat healtier

- Meditate

- Spend time with loved ones

- Take a social media break

- Spend time with yourself
