Mental Health 101

What is Mental Health?

Mental health refers to our condition in relation to our pyschological, emotional, and social well-being. It dictates our actions and inner thoughts - notably our ability to cope with stress and make cognitive decisions.

Among the most common disorders, however, are depression, schizophrenia, anxiety, and bipolar disorder. The causes are dependent on an individual's own body and their life experiences such as abuse, trauma, grief from loss, loneliness, or even their genes. However, an individual's mental health can also be impacted by their immediate environment - especially when it involves stress.

For example, individuals who are from low-income backgrounds or have strained interpersonal relationships are more likely to experience mental issues.

Importance of Mental Health

Mental health is important because it plays a significant role in our lives. It can dictate our thoughts and actions, and directly impact our physical, psychological, and emotional health.
